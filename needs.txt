Validation
	Conformity.  The desire to think and act like others, to be a part of a group.  Bipples feel more comfortable when others agree with their opinions and observations.  They'll either attempt to persuade others to their view, or reconsider their own beliefs to better fit in.
Importance
	Superiority, power.  To not only be part of a group, but to be a central part of it.  It involves having capabilities above others, making plans for others, and having others admire your beliefs and actions.
Discovery
	Curiosity, the desire to know things for the sake of knowing.
